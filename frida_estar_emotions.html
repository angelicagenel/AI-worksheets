Las Obras de Frida Kahlo - ESTAR + Emotions

ACTFL Level: Novice Mid

Instructions

Explore the emotions in Frida Kahlo's artwork. Complete the sentences using ESTAR + emotions. Choose the correct word for each blank. ESTAR describes how people or things appear in her paintings!

💭 ESTAR + Emotions (Temporary Feelings):

está triste - is/feels sad
está feliz - is/feels happy
está enojada - is/feels angry
está preocupada - is/feels worried
está tranquila - is/feels calm
está emocionada - is/feels excited

🎨 Cultural Understanding: Art as Emotional Expression

In Mexican culture, art shows feelings and tells stories. Frida Kahlo painted her emotions - when she was happy, sad, or angry. Her paintings help people understand how she felt. Mexican artists use art to share their feelings with others.

Cultural Information

Frida Kahlo was a famous Mexican artist. Her paintings show many different emotions. In her artwork, people and animals appear happy, sad, or angry. Her self-portraits show how she appears in different moments. When we look at her paintings today, we can see the emotions she painted. Her art teaches us that paintings can show feelings.
